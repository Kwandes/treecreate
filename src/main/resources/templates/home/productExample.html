Product

Unique wood design. With several options of changing design, fonts and text, every product becomes unique and personal. In just a few minutes you can create your own family tree and get it laser-cut into a beautiful piece of oak wood.

Sizes

After creating the design for your family tree, you are able to select the size you wish to purchase the tree in. We have a selection of sizes that include:
20 x 20 cm - 495kr
25 x 25 cm - 695kr
30 x 30 cm - 995kr
In case you wish to purchase a different size, you can always request it by sending us an email. We will respond as quickly as possible and give you the best price we can offer depending on your request.

Package

When you purchase a tree desing you are automatically planting a tree through TeamTrees.org. The packaging is made with a zero procent waste agenda. 0% plastic and 100% reused FSC certified paper. You also receive a treecreate keychain as well as a wooden stand that matches your family tree.
